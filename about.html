The Science of SOMA

SOMA Kombucha is where biology meets flavor. Our probiotic drink is crafted using a symbiotic culture of bacteria and yeast (SCOBY) that ferments organic green tea and sugar into a tangy, sparkling beverage.

Fermentation & Microbes

During fermentation, yeast transforms sugar into ethanol and CO₂, while acetic acid bacteria convert ethanol into organic acids such as acetic and gluconic acid. These natural processes result in a low-sugar, low-alcohol (~0.5%) drink rich in beneficial microbes like Brettanomyces and Komagataeibacter.

Why Kombucha Supports Gut Health

A balanced gut microbiome contributes to immune regulation, mood, and metabolism. The live cultures and organic acids in kombucha promote healthy gut flora and can help reduce sugar cravings by balancing blood glucose response.

Our Brewing Philosophy

We prioritize slow fermentation, natural carbonation, and sustainable sourcing. Each batch is brewed in Maastricht using purified water, organic tea, and fruit infusions — no artificial additives, ever.

Our Vision

We aim to redefine soda culture by merging scientific integrity with natural ingredients — inspiring people to “return to source” through mindful, probiotic refreshment.
