SOMA Kombucha was established in 2025 at the heart of Maastricht. This is the soda that loves your gut. Made with three ingredients, our focus is on health, simplicity, and authenticity. Every sip is a joyous experience full of soul and rich history taken from our ancestors.

Fermentation is an ancient process, and Kombucha was known as the Elixir of Life, supporting our health by detoxifying the body. The return to source aims to commemorate this experience and bring it to the masses. At SOMA Kombucha, we believe in a better future starting from each individual — and what other way to a better future than through better health: the fundamental backbone of each individual and thus society.

SOMA Kombucha’s logo has a unique backstory. The bike represents the start of the journey, during which I decided to take a trip to IKEA to buy the first several batches of glass bottles after registering the company. The journey took roughly 24 km and 2 hours. The bike used to transport the first batch of bottles has since been used in several promotion gigs.

Our company is dedicated to simplicity while also bettering itself. We do this by standardizing our fermentation process and participating in ongoing scientific research. This is elegance, class, and boldness at its finest, with everyone taken into account. Our research focuses on the gut-brain axis — essentially the way fermentation can potentially aid in helping the mental health of individuals.

Check out our blog for our latest research. Join us in the return to the source!
