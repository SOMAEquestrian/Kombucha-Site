About SOMA Kombucha

At SOMA Kombucha, we believe that what we drink can support both body and mind. With mental health challenges on the rise and diets high in sugar contributing to inflammation and cognitive strain, choosing healthier beverages is more important than ever.

Our kombucha is a naturally sparkling, probiotic drink brewed in Maastricht. Using high-quality organic green tea, white sugar, and a living SCOBY, we ferment our kombucha over 2–3 weeks, followed by a short carbonation period. This creates a complex, lightly acidic flavor with minimal alcohol (~0.5%), rich in beneficial bacteria such as Brettanomyces and Komagataeibacter.

We currently offer two flavors: Classic and Mixed Berry, in 500mL or 1L glass bottles. Available online, at local markets, and in select stores in Maastricht.

SOMA Kombucha aims to bring a healthier alternative to the carbonated beverage market. By creating kombucha that rivals the taste of soda, we encourage people to replace high-sugar soft drinks with a natural, probiotic option. Our vision includes scaling production and exploring bioengineered flavors while maintaining quality.

Our Mission: Reconnect people with natural, healthful beverages — The Return to Source.
