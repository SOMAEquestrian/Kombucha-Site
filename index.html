About Our Kombucha in Maastricht

The epitome of symbiosis of life

SOMA Kombucha is brewed at home in Maastricht using high-quality organic green tea, sugar, and a living SCOBY (Symbiotic Culture Of Bacteria and Yeast). Our berry blend is infused with a mix of different berries for an energizing burst of flavor. Every bottle is raw, unpasteurized, and alive with probiotics. Bottles can be returned for €2 refund.
