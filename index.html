About Our Kombucha

SOMA Kombucha is brewed at home using high-quality organic green tea, natural cane sugar, and a living SCOBY (Symbiotic Culture Of Bacteria and Yeast). Our berry blend is infused with a medley of hand-selected berries for an energizing burst of flavor. Every bottle is raw, unpasteurized, and alive with probiotics.
